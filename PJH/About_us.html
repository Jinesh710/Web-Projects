About Us



Located in the heart of South Mumbai, "Pancharatna Jalebi House" is one of the leading snacks spot. It was started by Mr. Bakulesh Gandhi in 1986 with a humble beginning.

With the rich experience, we offer cleanest and best quality products to suit the contemporary taste. The taste of each and every item is looked after personally by specialist to carry rich taste.

We treat every individual customer special and meeting their requirement is our prime focus. We will carry forward goodwill of "Pancharatna Jalebi House" quality and taste to constantly improvise our products.

Today "Pancharatna Jalebi House", a synonym to commitment to quality, uses state of art technique in manufacture of savoury and snacks to gain trust of our customers.
