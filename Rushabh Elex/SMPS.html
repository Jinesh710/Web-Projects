SMPS Enclosures are available with and without wall mounting clamps. These enclosures are specially designed for power supplies of camaras, these can also used for general purpose project housings, laptop charges and other SMPS chargers. Ocean make SMPS enclosures are moulded in black color ABS plastic. They have rectangle holes on two sides which can be used to take cables in and out of the enclosures.

SMPS ENCLOSURES



Dimensions of SMP-100R enclosure:

Description:
External dimensions 100x50x35 (mm).
Wall thickness of enclosure is 2.5 mm.
You will get 3 mm size below the PCB.
If PCB thickness is 1.6mm then you will get 25.40 mm size above PCB.
General Tolerance is .2 mm.

Dimensions of SMP-120R enclosure:

Description:
External dimensions 120x60x40 (mm).
Wall thickness of enclosure is 2.5 mm.
You will get 3 mm size below the PCB.
If PCB thickness is 1.6mm then you will get 30.40 mm size above PCB.
General Tolerance is .2 mm.
