Ocean make hand held enclosures are molded in black color, ABS plastic. These enclosures are available with and without battery compartment. Large display area facilitates to fix display at desired position. Hand held enclosures have plastic stand at the back to rest on table top in tilted position, they can be also hanged on wall.

HAND HELD ENCLOSURE WITH 9V BATTERY COMPARTMENT(SAY-99)



Description:
Ocean make enclosure SAY-99 is ideal for hand held instruments.
Display cutout designed for mounting digital/analog LCD displays.
Display cutout size is 65x52 mm.
Snap-in battery compartment lids for quick battery replacement.
Sufficient area below PCB to accommodate plugs & switches.
Tilt foot bar for ergonomic desktop applications.
Built-in provision for wall hanging.
Mounting of membrane keypads is also possible (70x80 mm).
Internal screw pillars for mounting PCBs.
Available in black color and size is 169x82x30 (mm).
