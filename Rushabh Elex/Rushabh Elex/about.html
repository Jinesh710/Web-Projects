About Us

Incorporated in the year 1995, at Mumbai (Maharashtra,India), “Rushabh Electronics”, is a trading company catering to Electronic Instrument Industries. We are Authorized Distributor for following manufacturers, Viz


  • Integral Systems and Components Pvt. Ltd., Bangalore
  • PIRI Electrols Pvt. Ltd. Mumbai
  • Electroconnect Systems, Mumbai

All of our principals are ISO–9001 Approved. We offer full range of Toggle Switches, Tiny Toggle Switches ( Series 50 ), Thumb Wheel & Pushwheel Switches, Rotary Switches, Push Button Micro Reset Switches from M / S Integral Systems, Banglore. We also offer full range of DIN Rail Enclosure, Terminal Blocks from M / S PIRI Electrols, Mumbai and offer full range of Panel Mounting DIN Size Enclosures as well as DIN Rail Enclosures from M / S Electroconnect Systems, Mumbai All our offered products are manufactured using the best quality raw material and the most advanced techniques by our principals in order to meet the Stringent Quality Standard. Our products are highly accepted by our clients for their because of quality and consistency.


By following our set quality standards, we have been able to satisfy variant requirements of our clients that are spread all across the country. The offered range of products is manufactured in complete compliance with the defined international quality standards and norms and offer provide utmost satisfaction to our clients. Being a customer - centric organization, our prime objective is to achieve the utmost contentment of our esteemed clients by providing them with the quality assured range of products. Moreover, we deliver our products in a qualitative packaging material in order to ensure safe transit of products at clients' end. Our easy payment modes, customer - centric approach, transparent dealings, competitive price range and fair business strategies enable us to place our firm at the zenith of success and help us to obtain a wide clientele all across the country.


Under the experienced leadership of “Mr. Deepak Zaveri” (Proprietor), we have achieved a respectable position in the industry. It’s his guidance that makes us capable to utilize our available resources in an effective and efficient manner.
