DIN RAIL ENCLOSURE EC-7522

Ocean make DIN RAIL ENCLOSURES are used to enclose electronic instruments for measurement, control and instrumentation applications and these electronic instruments are mounted in side the panel on standard DIN RAILS.

Description: Ocean make Din Rail Enclosure EC-7522 is available with 8 terminals. This enclosure is din rail mounting and also wall mounting. Overall dimensions are (D=75 X W=22 X H=100). Finger guards of this enclosure are available in orange and blue colors. Both single and dual knob instruments are possible to make in this enclosure.
